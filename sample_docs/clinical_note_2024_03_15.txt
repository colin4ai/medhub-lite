2. Start gabapentin 300mg three times daily for neuropathic pain

4. Work restrictions:

   - Light duty work only
5. Follow-up in 2 weeks to assess progress
6. Consider epidural steroid injection if conservative management fails

PROGNOSIS:
Guarded. Many patients with similar herniation respond to conservative treatment within 6-12 weeks. However, given the degree of radiculopathy and functional limitations, surgical intervention may be necessary if symptoms do not improve with conservative management.

DISABILITY CONSIDERATIONS:
Patient is currently unable to perform his regular job duties as a warehouse worker, which requires frequent lifting of objects up to 50 pounds, prolonged standing, and repetitive bending. Current restrictions prevent return to usual occupation. Patient may be suitable for light-duty sedentary work with accommodations.

Return to work timeline: Estimated 8-12 weeks for light duty work, 16-24 weeks for full duty if conservative management successful.
